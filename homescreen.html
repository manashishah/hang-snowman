Instructions

  1. Choose a category from Countries, Superheroes, or Animals.
  2. Select Easy, Medium, or Hard for your chosen category.
  3. A statement related to the word will be provided.
  4. Guess the word using the given hint within the time limit.
  5. Earn points based on the difficulty level of the word you guessed.
  6. Choose your next difficulty level for the same category or switch to another category.
  7. The game ends when the timer reaches 0 or when you decide to stop playing.
  8. Your total score is the sum of the points earned for each correctly guessed word.
  9. The highest score achieved during the game will be prominently displayed at the end.
  10. Beat the highest score by successfully guessing words in all categories & difficulty levels.

Have fun challenging yourself and testing your knowledge across different categories!
