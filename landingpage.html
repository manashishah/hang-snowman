Every wrong guess by players tore him apart, now, he is but a shadow of his former self, a spectral figure torn by countless failures, each wrong letter adding to his eternal winter.

It's your turn to face this haunted snowman. With each letter, you hold his fate: to restore his lost soul or condemn him further. Will you be his savior, rekindling the warmth he once knew, or will you be the one to leave him as a frozen monument, a relic of a soul that once danced in the snow?
