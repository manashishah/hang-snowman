Hang-SnowMan

Categories:

  • Countries
  • Superheroes
  • Animals

Difficulty Modes:

  • Easy
  • Medium
  • Hard

Time Limit:

5 minutes

Scoring:

  • Easy: 3 points
  • Medium: 5 points
  • Hard: 7 points

Hints:

You will be provided with a hint for each word.

Highest Score:

The highest score will be displayed at the end of the game.

Instructions:

  1. Choose a category from Countries, Superheroes, or Animals.
  2. Select Easy, Medium, or Hard for your chosen category.
  3. A statement related to the word will be provided.
  4. Guess the word using the given hint within the time limit.
  5. Earn points based on the difficulty level of the word you guessed.
  6. Try to beat the highest score by successfully guessing words in all categories and difficulty levels.
